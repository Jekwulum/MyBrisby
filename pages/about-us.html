Our Purpose

Our goal at myBrisby is to provide new students moving to Brisbane with all the support and help they need. All new students to the city are encouraged to have a sense of belonging in an environment that we work hard to build. Our aim is for every student to feel encouraged and empowered to excel in both their academic and extracurricular efforts. We are dedicated to offering the tools and advice required to make the move as easy as possible because we recognize that moving to a new city can be overwhelming.

Welcome to myBrisby! At myBrisby, we are committed to making your transition to Brisbane as seamless and enjoyable as possible. We understand that starting university or college in a new city can be overwhelming, which is why we are here to help. Our primary focus is on assisting new students with accommodation, support services, airport pickup, and community linking. We believe that having a comfortable place to live, access to helpful resources, a smooth airport arrival, and a supportive community can significantly enhance your overall experience as a student.

Accommodation: Finding suitable accommodation is crucial, and we are here to guide you through the process. We provide information on various housing options, including on-campus residences, off-campus rentals, and homestays. Our goal is to help you find a safe and comfortable place that suits your preferences and budget.

Support Services: We understand that adjusting to a new environment can be challenging. That's why we offer a range of support services to ensure you have the assistance you need. From academic support and counseling services to health and well-being resources, we are here to support your personal and academic success.

Airport Pickup: Arriving at a new airport can be daunting, especially when you're unfamiliar with the city. Our airport pickup service ensures a warm welcome as soon as you land. We will arrange for a friendly and knowledgeable representative to greet you, assist with your luggage, and help you get settled in your new accommodation.

Community Linking: Building connections and forming friendships with fellow students is an essential part of your university experience. Our community linking program connects you with international communities and student organizations, providing opportunities to meet people from different backgrounds, share experiences, and create a supportive network.
